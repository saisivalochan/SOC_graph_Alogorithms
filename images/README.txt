Where to put your AKG slide screenshots:

  images/week1/  ← Week 1 concept images
  images/week2/  ← Week 2 concept images
  ... etc up to week7/

How to use:
  1. Snip a slide from your AKG slides (Windows: Win+Shift+S → save as PNG)
  2. Drop it in the matching week's folder
  3. In data.js, set the concept's "image" field to the path, e.g.:
       image: "images/week1/two-pointers.png"
  4. Save → refresh the concept page.

Naming tip: use lowercase with dashes, e.g. "binary-search.png".
PNG and JPG both work.
